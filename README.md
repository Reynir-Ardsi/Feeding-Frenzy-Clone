# Feeding Frenzy Clone

A survival-based underwater feeding game with progressive enemy difficulty and resource management mechanics.

## Game Description

In this Feeding Frenzy clone, you control a fish navigating an underwater environment filled with various other fish species. Your primary objective is to **survive as long as possible** while managing two critical resources: **health** and **hunger**.

### Core Concept

You begin each session with limited health and must constantly eat other fish to maintain your hunger level. As your hunger depletes over time, your health begins to drain, forcing you into a resource management scenario. Eat the smaller, docile fish to stay alive, but beware of the dangerous predators lurking in the deeper waters.

## Controls

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **Mouse Movement** | Move your fish towards the cursor location |
| **Left Click / Hold** | Dash in the direction of the cursor (high speed, short duration) |
| **Start Button** | Begin the game or restart after death |


## Known Issues & Limitations

- **Swordfish AI**: May occasionally become stuck at screen edges during chase behavior
- **Collision Detection**: Bomb fish explosion radius can sometimes cause unintended interactions with distant entities
- **Performance**: Game may experience frame rate drops with max fish count (40) all active simultaneously
- **Mobile Support**: Currently designed for mouse-based input; touch controls are not implemented
- **Sound**: No audio implemented (visual-only experience)
- **Pause Feature**: Game cannot be paused during active gameplay—only restart option available
- **Difficulty Settings**: No adjustable difficulty or custom spawn rates currently available
